Walking is an everyday activity!

It helps us go to the grocery store, to our classes, to the other side of the city if we are so pleased. Walking helps us stay in shape and stay healthy. But what if it could do more for us? What if walking could give us power?

Our product does just that.

It takes the compression energy generated on your footwear and converts it to stored electrical power so you can use it to charge up your portable devices!
