Our Team

Neil John

Chief Executive Officer

Neil has spent most of his life as a sneakerhead, always wanting to design his own shoe. After spending multiple years on projects related to sustainible energy Neil directed his energy to the goal of building an energy harvesting shoe. As a dancer and an engineer, Neil wants to ensure that the product he delivers is something that he would use when on a walk or on a stage.

Koushik Neogi

Chief Technology Officer

Koushik is a mechanical engineer by career who specializes in simulating and optimizing mechanical and electrical systems. First prototyped in a senior design project, Koushik has been working to optimize and create the best product for out users.
